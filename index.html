Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

+

You can book a service by selecting your preferred service, filling out the booking form, and confirming via WhatsApp. Our team will contact you to confirm the appointment.

Are your stylists verified?

+

Yes, all our stylists are professionally trained, certified, and background-verified. They carry their own sanitized equipment and follow strict hygiene protocols.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. Last-minute cancellations may incur a small fee.

Do you provide services in all areas?

+

We currently serve major areas in Bangalore, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, and Delhi. Enter your address during booking to check if we serve your location.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

+

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with our service, we'll either send another stylist or provide a full refund.
